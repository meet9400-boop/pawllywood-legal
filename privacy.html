Privacy Policy — Pawllywood Uploader

Last updated: 2026-07-05

This Privacy Policy describes how the Pawllywood content-uploading tool ("the App") handles data. The App is a private automation utility operated by the owner of the Pawllywood social media channels to publish the owner's own original short-form videos to the owner's own accounts on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

Information We Access

  • Account authorization tokens. When you connect a TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube account, the platform issues an OAuth access token. The App stores this token locally on the operator's own computer solely to publish videos to that same account.
  • Basic account profile. Public profile fields (username, display name, account ID) are read once to confirm the correct account is connected.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or process data belonging to any third party or any other user.
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • The App has no server and no database; there is no cloud storage of your data.

How Tokens Are Used

Access tokens are used exclusively to call the official publishing APIs of TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube to upload the operator's own videos. Tokens are stored only on the operator's local machine in a file that is never shared or transmitted anywhere except to the issuing platform's own API endpoints.

Data Retention & Deletion

Tokens remain on the local machine until the operator deletes them or they expire. To revoke access, disconnect the App from within your TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube account settings, and delete the local token file.

Content Disclosure

Videos published by the App may contain AI-generated imagery. This is disclosed on each platform using that platform's AI-content labeling features.
